# My Personal Portfolio – PLP Academy Hackathon 1

This is my submission for **PLP Academy July 2025 Cohort Hackathon 1: Portfolio Challenge**.  
It is a **single-page personal portfolio website** built using **vanilla HTML and CSS**, following the instructions in the Hackathon [README](https://github.com/MuriithiEric/July2025Cohort-Hackathon1?tab=readme-ov-file#july-2025-cohort-hackathon-1-portfolio-challenge-).

---

## 📖 About This Project

This portfolio showcases:

- **About Me** – Who I am and what drives me
- **Programming Languages** – Languages I am currently proficient in
- **Educational Background** – My academic history and a link to download my CV
- **Interests** – My professional interests and areas of focus
- **Projects** – Links to some of my past projects
- **Contact Form** – A simple form to reach me

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **HTML5**  
- **CSS3**  

No frameworks or JavaScript were used, as per the Hackathon rules.
